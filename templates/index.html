WHAT DO WE DO FOR YOU?

iEat empowers you to save about 30 minutes by placing an order for the food online while travelling to the restaurant or before even leaving the comfort of your home.

On a mission to give you an unmatched food ordering experience from the restaurant's dining table, anywhere in Srinagar without making those awkward signs to get waiters attention for order or any request.

With iEat, enjoy contactless, wait-less dining and bid goodbye to those unhygienic menu cards.

HOW DOES IT WORK?


6 Easy Steps

DINE OUT

  • 1
    Navigate to iEat mobile application or website and click on Dine out.
  • 2
    Select the best restaurant nearby or perform a search for your favourite restaurant.
  • 3
    View deals and menu , select items to order and enter your arrival time.
  • 4
    Select payment option to place the order.
  • 5
    Head towards the restaurant on your predefined arrival time.
  • 6
    Get served under 150 seconds!

E-WAITER

  • 1
    Make your way to your favourite restaurant and be seated.
  • 2
    Navigate to iEat application or website and click on E-waiter.
  • 3
    Scan the table QR code.
  • 4
    Select items to order from menu OR request extras (water, tissue paper, spoons/plates etc.).
  • 5
    Select payment option to place the order.
  • 6
    Get served as soon as the Kitchen accepts and processes the order.
